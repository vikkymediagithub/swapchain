Support Articles and FAQs

How does PixoDex OTC work?

PixoDex OTC crypto exchange accomplishes high-volume transactions in a legal and secure way. Trades are facilitated swiftly and discreetly, facilitating exchanges over one million Euro. The best characteristic of the OTC crypto exchange is that such substantial transactions do not cause noticeable fluctuations in market prices and eliminate the risks associated with price volatility or slippage.

What fees does PixoDex have?

PixoDex allows businesses and individuals to pay and accept payments in crypto, as well as reduce processing cost via lower fees. With traditional banking, fees can go as high as 5% while the PixoDex transaction fee is less than 1%. Moreover, we don't charge any setup or monthly hidden fees.

How do I withdraw exchanged coins?

After a successful trade, the exchanged coins are moved to your spot wallet. You can easily withdraw to your preferred digital wallet by proceeding to request for withdrawal. Withdrawals are processed instantly.
